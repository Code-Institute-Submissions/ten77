About Us

Ten77 was set up in the summer of 2018 by Nick, an ex police officer and experienced police dog handler. After 30 years of service Nick retired from the police force and was looking for a new venture to combine his love of animals, photography and the outdoors all in one! Nick began by photographing the pets of his friends and family and the results were so professional Ten77 was born. Based in Teesside, Nick has a huge love for the area and often incorporates it's local beauty into his outdoor shoots. Nick is an outgoing, friendly and enthusiastic person who has always been actively involved in the local community, first as a community police officer and now as the regular photographer at the monthly community dog walking group. So if you're based in the North East and have a pet you want some cracking photographs of then please get in touch!

Welcome to Ten77 Dog Photography - we want to create photographs to give you everlasting memories of your furry friends!

Ten77 was set up in the summer of 2018 by Nick, an ex police officer and experienced police dog handler. After 30 years of service Nick retired from the police force and was looking for a new venture to combine his passion for animals with his love of photography. Nick began by photographing the pets of his friends and family and the results were so professional Ten77 was born!

Nick doesn't bite - ask him a question!
